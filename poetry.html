Some Poems I have written


Wires

Aritya Vitendra Arjunan

Flexible wires give us energy,

And communication, and sound, and transport.

They are useful in every way,

Like air, water, and food.

Wires brighten your mood,

By allowing you to drive to the bay.

Wires are serious; they only provide information (sometimes),

They never laugh, they never cry,

They only do your work for thy.



Water

Aritya Vitendra Arjunan

The lapping of polluted water

Is slaughtered for the creatures

Are suffocating, the pollution cackling.


The lapping of clean water

Is beautiful and pristine.

The fish and the crabs can be seen.

Fish flying through the air.

Dolphins leaping is no longer rare.

The waves breaking on the shore

Sounds happy, on the third octave.



The Tree

Aritya Vitendra Arjunan

The green Tree slowly moverd in the wind

Using Sun as energy for growing

How? By photosynthesis

The animals are eating It but the Tree does not mind.


It is always very kind.

The magic of chlorophyll, turning it green.

It even works on a bean.

And forever the Tree stands.



Chlorophyll

Aritya Vitendra Arjunan

Chlorophyll
